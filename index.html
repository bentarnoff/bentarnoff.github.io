ben tarnoff

I'm a tech worker, writer, and co-founder of Logic Magazine.
My email address is ben AT bentarnoff.com.
My Twitter is @bentarnoff.
And I have a very irregular newsletter.
You can find a complete list of my pieces below. Also, I have a new book.
It's called Internet for the People, and it's about how the modern internet came to be, why it's so broken, and what might make it less broken.
You can order it from Verso or Bookshop.
